HOW TO COOK DELICIOUS CHICKEN?
  1. Take 500gms of Chicken
  2. Add spices
    • 2 tablespoon of (Red chilly)lal mirch
    • 1/2 tablespoon haldi powder
    • 2 tablespoon cummin powder
    • 2 tablespoon corriander powder
  3. Add 2 cups curd
  4. Marinate well and Leave for 30 1/2mins
